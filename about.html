Admiralty's bakert inc. was founded in the year 2018 and was initiated by the owners strong love for pasteries and baking. It was founded with an aim to cure poverty and hunger in the country and also to eradicate mal-nourishment in the society. It has been approved by NAFDAC after thorough examination of the food content health wise and in other areas.

ABI has futher expounded to several parts of the country and are making waves in the confectionary industries. In partne rship with Manda Foodies, we have established branches in Lagos, Enugu, Imo, Anambra, Benue, Kano, and Abuja respectively.
